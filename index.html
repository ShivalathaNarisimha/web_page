Wada

Indgrideints

  • Urad Dal
  • Oil
  • Basic herbs & spices

recipe

  1. Soak urad dal for 12hrs and then ferment it
  2. Add basic veges,spices &mix well
  3. Deep fry it in hot oil on medium flame for 4min

Masala Dosa

Indgrideints

  • 100 gm Urad Dal & 150 rice
  • Oil
  • Basic herbs & spices

recipe

  1. Soak urad dal & rice for 12hrs and then ferment it
  2. Add spices &mix well
  3. Spread batter on hot pan and soread patato mixture on it
  4. Your deicious Dosa is ready
